Do you know about us

While there are countless furniture stores around and to choose from, Rhinoland Furniture, based in Kathmandu with a showroom located at Bishalnagar is established with the commitment of high quality service to those looking for the type of furniture they want. We make and supply furniture as per the requirement and taste of our customers and clients at our own workshop with skilled craftemen we have inhouse.

Each piece is made with opulence and longevity in mind, and as such we only use the finest timbers and other building materials.
